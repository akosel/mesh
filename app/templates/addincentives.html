What is this?

At Mesh, we know that sometimes people need extra motivation to accomplish their goals, so we have added in a simple way of doing that. You and your friends can agree on a group of penalties that you have to follow through on if you don't complete tasks in time. We set the default to donating to a charity of your choice, but you can change it to anything. For example, you can say "Buy a bottle of wine for Bob" or "Run three miles". The idea is that you can be creative; the goal is that you should avoid missing tasks and this provides a fun, optional way of doing that.
